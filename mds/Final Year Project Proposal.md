### **Title:**

**Dynamic Stock Price Prediction System**

---
 
### **Proposal Category:**

**Public**

---

### **Project Description:**

This project aims to develop a **Dynamic Stock Price Prediction System** that leverages **machine learning and deep learning** techniques, specifically the **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory)** neural network model — a type of **Recurrent Neural Network (RNN)** designed for sequential and time-series data.

The system will **regularly analyze live stock market data** from reliable sources (such as Yahoo Finance or Alpha Vantage APIs) to forecast short-term and long-term stock prices. By continuously learning from historical and real-time data, the system will help investors and analysts make **data-driven decisions** based on trend predictions, volatility analysis, and pattern recognition.

The model will automatically **fetch, preprocess, and visualize stock data**, train the **LSTM** on recent historical prices, and then **predict future prices dynamically**. A **web-based dashboard** will display current prices, predicted prices, performance graphs, and confidence intervals.

---

### **Objectives:**

1. To collect and preprocess real-time and historical stock market data.
    
2. To implement an **LSTM-based predictive model** capable of forecasting stock prices.
    
3. To compare LSTM with traditional models (e.g., ARIMA or Linear Regression).
    
4. To visualize predictions using an **interactive dashboard** (using Streamlit, Dash, or Flask).
    
5. To evaluate prediction accuracy using metrics like **RMSE, MAE, and MAPE**.
    
6. To build an **automated system** that updates predictions as new data arrives.
    

---

- **Programming Language:** Python
